How to find us.

We are located in Beach Village on beautiful Jekyll Island, GA. We are situated between the Westin Hotel and the Convention Center. Parking is available at the back of the store for your convenience. We look forward to seeing you soon! Click here for Google Maps!

Beer

We are a full service Beverage Center for all your beverage needs! If craft beers are your thing then check out our large selection of beers. We proudly carry local craft beers from Georgia, Florida, Bama, and all around! You can even make your own six pack just for fun. We also carry non-alcoholic beer and ice too!

Wine

Ladies we thought about you too! If craft beer isn't your thing, you can choose from our vast selection of wine. We even have beach ready wine in the can or ready to drink cocktails. Just open and enjoy, no bottle opener necessary! Checkout our assortment of glassware for those special celebrations. We also feature local Georgia wine.

Spirits & Cigars

For the Gentlemen we have the largest selection of Cigars on Jekyll Island. We also have cigar cutters and torch lighters as well. They will pair perfectly with your favorite Scotch, Cognac, Bourbon, or Whisky. We also carry many local spirits from Georgia and have plenty of mixers to choose from.
